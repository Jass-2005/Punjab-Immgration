KEY REQUIREMENTS

What You Need to Show

Valid Passport with sufficient validity.
Proof of sufficient funds to cover your trip.
Strong ties to your home country (job, family, property).
A clear travel itinerary and purpose of visit.
Invitation letter (if visiting family or for business).
HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much money do I need in my bank account?

There is no exact amount. You must demonstrate that you have enough funds to comfortably cover your flights, accommodation, and daily expenses for the entire duration of your trip without needing to work.

What are "strong ties to home country"?

This is the most important part. You need to prove you have compelling reasons to return home, such as a stable job, family, property, or business. This assures the visa officer that you will not overstay your visa.

Can I work on a visitor visa?

No. A visitor visa strictly prohibits you from engaging in paid employment. For business visitors, activities are limited to meetings, negotiations, and conferences, not active labor.
