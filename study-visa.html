OUR PROCESS

The 4-Step Study Visa Process

1. Counselling

We assess your academic profile and help you choose the right course and country that aligns with your career goals.

2. Offer Letter

We manage your university applications to secure your admission (CAS/LOA) from the best institutions.

3. Documentation

Expert guidance on funding, GICs, medicals, and writing a strong Statement of Purpose (SOP).

4. Visa & Departure

We handle the final visa lodging and provide comprehensive pre-departure briefings to prepare you.
