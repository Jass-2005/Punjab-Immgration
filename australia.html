WHY CHOOSE AUSTRALIA?

The Sunburnt Country

Australia is famous for its sunny beaches, vibrant cities, and unique wildlife. It offers a high standard of living and a booming economy with a strong demand for skilled professionals.

The Australian education system is globally respected, and its clear, points-based immigration system provides a direct pathway to permanent residency for many students and workers.

Amazing Lifestyle

Enjoy a high quality of life with a perfect balance of work and leisure.

Work Opportunities

High wages and a strong demand for skilled trades and professionals.

Clear PR Pathways

A structured, points-based system for permanent residency.

YOUR OPTIONS

Pathways to Australia

Student (Subclass 500)

Study full-time at a top Australian university, college, or TAFE institution.

Skilled (Subclass 189)

A points-tested visa for skilled workers who are not sponsored by an employer.

Skilled (Subclass 190)

A permanent visa for skilled workers who are nominated by an Australian state.

Visitor (Subclass 600)

For visiting Australia as a tourist, for a business meeting, or to visit family.
