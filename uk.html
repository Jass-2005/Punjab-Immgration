Destination: United Kingdom

Home of History, Culture, and World-Class Education.

WHY CHOOSE THE UK?

A Tradition of Excellence

The United Kingdom offers a unique blend of rich history and modern innovation. Its universities are legendary, and its cities are global hubs for finance, tech, and the creative arts.

With the new Graduate Route visa, international students can now stay and work (or look for work) for up to two years after completing their degree, making it one of the most attractive destinations today.

World-Class Education

Study at prestigious universities like Oxford, Cambridge, and LSE.

Graduate Route (PSW)

Stay and work in the UK for 2 years post-graduation (3 years for PhD).

Historic Culture

Live and work in a country with a rich, globally-influential history.

YOUR OPTIONS

Pathways to the UK

Student Visa

For students who have an offer (CAS) from a licensed UK university or institution.

Skilled Worker Visa

For those who have a job offer from a UK employer with a sponsorship licence.

Visitor Visa

For tourism, visiting family, or short-term business meetings and conferences.

Family & Spouse Visa

Join your partner or family members who are settled in the United Kingdom.
